üí° About Us

Who We Are

Founded by Mr. Mahender Jaiswal, Vijaya Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to improving lives through sustainable development. Our mission is to empower marginalized communities by promoting education, health awareness, environmental protection, and women's empowerment.

Our Mission

To bring lasting social change by empowering individuals and communities with knowledge, opportunity, and support.

Our Vision

A world where every person lives with dignity, equality, and access to essential resources for growth and wellbeing.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion & Empathy
  • Transparency & Integrity
  • Sustainability
  • Collaboration
  • Commitment to Excellence

ü§ù Our Work

1. Education for All

We believe education is the foundation of progress. Vijaya Foundation runs literacy drives, scholarship programs, and school support initiatives to ensure that every child gets a chance to learn and dream big.

2. Health & Hygiene

Through free medical camps, health awareness workshops, and nutrition support programs, we aim to improve community health and well-being in rural and urban areas alike.

3. Women Empowerment

We conduct training sessions, livelihood programs, and self-help group initiatives to help women achieve financial independence and social equality.

4. Environment & Sustainability

Our green initiatives include tree plantation drives, waste management awareness, and clean-up campaigns to build a sustainable and eco-friendly future.
